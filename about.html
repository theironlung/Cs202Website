About

I am a student studying Graphic Design at Western Washington University. Through art people can share mutual understanding of ideas. Design’s intrigue for me is its ability to convey meaning and emotion. Language is not a barrier when communicating visually. Powerful design can spark a movement or lay the foundation for a business’s or event’s reputation. I am interested in both computing and art. I feel design brings elements from each field, the versatility of computing and the expressiveness of art. I hope to find a career in design that allows me to combine my passions in computing and art. Good design inspires people, is beautiful, and is useful in any industry. I want to make a difference by making good design.
